About Me

Hello, I'm Joe!

My earliest and most impactful memories are those involving video games. From my first console (a Nintendo DS!), my passion for games has only grown. This passion inspired me to become part of the magic that made my own childhood so special by becoming a game designer.

I believe that creating a great game starts with caring about your project and the player on the other end.

My love for creating & designing experiences led me into my first large role with Club Penguin Rewritten, a fan server of a popular MMO called Club Penguin. Although it was a fan server, it allowed me to create content for an audience of over five million registered users.

The experience helped me learn a variety of new skills which were important to my development as a game designer.

After I left Club Penguin Rewritten in 2020, I continued to develop my skills through private contracting work. This allowed me to find new specialized interests that I was able to nurture during my time at Bournemouth University, where I have now graduated as of July 2022.
