About Me


Hello! I'm Joseph, a video game designer from the UK, with a love of video games.

Since the day that I recieved my first game console, my passion for games has never stopped growing. My earliest and most impactful memories are those involving video games. They lead me to be a part of the magic that made my childhood so special by becoming a game designer. I believe that the first step to creating a good game is caring about your project, and the people who play it.

My love for creating & designing experiences led me into my first large role with Club Penguin Rewritten, a fan server of a popular MMO called Club Penguin. Although this was just a fan server, it allowed me to create content for an audience of over five million registered users - which in turn, helped me learn a variety of new skills which were important to my development as a game designer.

After I left Club Penguin Rewritten in 2020, I continued to develop my skills through private contracting work. This allowed me to find new specialized interests that I was able to nuture during my time at Bournemouth University. I have graduated as of July 2022; however, my grades will not be finalized until September 2022.
