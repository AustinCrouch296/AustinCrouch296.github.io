Umbra

Umbra was the product created for my final year dissertation study at Bournemouth University, "Designing a Horror Puzzle Game in Virtual Reality Using a Heartbeat Monitor". I created the game to research the best ways to make an immersive VR experience. While the game only contained puzzles, an eerie environment, and a few scripted events, my proposal was to use a heart monitor and player surveying to discover the games strengths and weaknesses.
